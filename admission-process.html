Eligibility Criteria

Requirements for admission to Cadet College Sanghar

Age Limit

Candidates must meet the following age requirements:

  • For Class 8: 12-14 years as of 1st April
  • For Class 9: 13-15 years as of 1st April
  • For Class 11: 15-17 years as of 1st April

Academic Qualification

Minimum academic requirements for admission:

  • For Class 8: Passed Class 7 with at least 60% marks
  • For Class 9: Passed Class 8 with at least 60% marks
  • For Class 11: Passed Class 10 with at least 60% marks

Medical Fitness

Candidates must be medically fit:

  • Free from any physical disability
  • Normal vision (6/6 without glasses)
  • Normal hearing capability
  • No chronic diseases

Nationality

Citizenship requirements:

  • Must be a citizen of Pakistan
  • Domicile of Sindh province preferred
  • Children of armed forces personnel given preference

Selection Process

Step-by-step procedure for admission

Application Submission

Submit the completed application form along with required documents before the deadline.

Deadline: March 31, 2025

Written Test

All applicants must appear for the entrance examination covering English, Mathematics, Urdu, and General Knowledge.

Date: April 15, 2025

Interview & Medical

Shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview and medical examination at the college campus.

Date: April 25-30, 2025

Final Selection

Final selection based on test results, interview performance, and medical fitness.

Announcement: May 10, 2025
