Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)

Cadet College Sanghar

A Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for the establishment of the Confucius Department at Cadet College Sanghar was signed on 25 Oct 25 at Cadet College Petaro. The MoU was signed by Cdre Faisal Iqbal Qazi SI(M), Principal, CCP and Cdre Zeeshan Ali SI(M), Principal CCS, Director for the Confucius Department, Prof. Siraj Ahmed Channa, Vice Principal (Admin) CCP and Prof. Feroz Gul Khoso, Director of Studies, CCS. The purpose of this agreement is to introduce both credit and non-credit Chinese language courses at Cadet College Sanghar. This collaboration marks a significant step toward enhancing international language learning and fostering academic cooperation.
