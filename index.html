Welcome to Cadet College Sanghar

Cadet College Sanghar inspires young cadets to become disciplined leaders and serve Pakistan with pride. Our unique curriculum blends rigorous academic training with the character-building discipline of a military institution, creating well-rounded individuals prepared for success in all walks of life.

Message from the Principal

At Cadet College Sanghar, we shape tomorrow's leaders with discipline, knowledge, and moral character. Our mission is to provide cadets with the best environment to excel in academics, physical training, and leadership, preparing them for a life of service to our beloved nation, Pakistan.

We believe in holistic development that balances academic rigor with character building, ensuring our cadets emerge as confident, capable, and compassionate individuals ready to make meaningful contributions to society.

Commodore Zeeshan Ali SI(M)

Our Mission

To impart quality education and shape young boys into principled leaders through conducive environment that supports personality grooming, physical fitness and character development with spirit of service towards the nation

Our Vision

To groom the youth, mainly from rural areas, by employing the best educational practices, sound physical training and core ethical values to develop them into good human beings, patriotic citizens and dynamic leaders
